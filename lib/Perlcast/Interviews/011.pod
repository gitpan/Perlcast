=head1 NAME

Perlcast Interview 011 - Interview with Andy Lester

=head1 ABSTRACT

Perlcast, the Perl podcast, back with an interview with Andy Lester. Andy is 
the new public relations representative for the Perl Foundation, a 2005 White 
Camel winner, creator of WWW::Mechanize, and an advocate for Perl Quality 
Assurance. Listen in for Andy’s take on Perl and a little side note on 
Google’s Summer of Code.

The audio of this interview can be found at 
L<Perlcast.com|http://www.perlcast.com/audio/Perlcast_Interview_011_Lester.mp3>.
